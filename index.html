about me

I officially began my web developer career after taking the Coding Bootcamp at the University of Toronto. The bootcamp focused on user interface and user experience design along with the modern MERN stack for web development.

Prior to that, my front end development was focused on copy and paste of existing code to match the existing website design. In addition, I have done back end development with PHP where I have created and updated modules for a shopping cart website.

I have always been exposed to a very detailed oriented environment. I have been a Business System Analyst since 2010 where I have been applying my skills to complex initiatives like moving customer data from obsolete to current systems and enhancements to an online transaction system. In fact, working on that online transaction system sparked my interest in upgrading my web design and web development skills.

The list in the work section is the sample of my completed and Work-In-Progress web development projects. All follow modern design along with responsive layout that looks good on all modern devices and browsers. To get in touch with me, fill out the form below or follow me on other social platforms.
